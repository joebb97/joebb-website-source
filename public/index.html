System design hints 2 Functionality: 2.1: Keep it simple - Do one thing at a time, do it well. Small, working, effective interfaces, they are contracts, shouldn’t try to do too much, don’t generalize - Get it right - Write specifications (then implement), document the abstract functionality provided by your application and its interfaces 2.2: Corollaries - Make it fast rather than general or powerful. - Don’t hide the power of the interface, clients should know what it does well.
